The Introduction

QPCRBox is a web application that helps anybody do anonymous, fast and super-simple QPCR results analysis. When you use QPCRBox we don't require any registration or email to confirm your identity. We offer you free tool which helps you understand your results data in amazingly short time. The results can be saved on your computer and used in next computation.

All what you need is a modern browser, an Internet connection. The rest is done by us.

For advanced use we offer free public computation API which can be easily integrated into your computation process flow.

Be smart and fast!

Secure

You and Your data are super-important for us. We don't need your registration or email! The trust you put in us should be based on facts that communication (as the data) are sent to us and from us SSL encrypted so nobody expect you and our server can see them.

We don't store results of your computation longer than you need! The data are only in computer temporary memory and after 1 hour will be erased without any chance to get back again. We believe that you know better where and how to store them.
